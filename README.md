# Market Expansion & Profitability Analysis — Cenia Enterprise (Excel Dashboard)

## Overview
This project presents an Excel-based market expansion and profitability analysis for **Cenia Enterprise**, a U.S.-based wholesale company specializing in office supplies, furniture, and technology accessories.

The objective of the analysis is to support leadership decision-making for Cenia Enterprise’s planned expansion into **North America and Western Europe by Q4 2027**, using historical sales and profitability data to identify high-performing regions, product categories, and customer segments.

---

## Business Context
Cenia Enterprise operates in a highly competitive wholesale environment where margins, logistics efficiency, and customer demand patterns directly influence expansion success.

This project answers key business questions such as:
- Which regions and markets drive the highest revenue and profit?
- Which product categories are most profitable?
- How do discounts impact overall profitability?
- What customer segments should be prioritized during expansion?

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts  
- Excel Formulas (IF, SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, Profit Margin calculations)  
- Excel Slicers for interactivity  
- PowerPoint (Executive presentation of insights)

---

## Dataset
The dataset includes historical sales transactions with fields such as:
- Order Date, Ship Date, Ship Mode  
- Region, Country, State, City  
- Customer Segment  
- Product Category & Sub-Category  
- Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discount  

The data spans **2014–2017**, providing sufficient history for trend and performance analysis.

---

## Project Methodology
1. **Data Preparation**
   - Created a backup of the original dataset to preserve data integrity.

2. **Data Cleaning**
   - Removed duplicate records.
   - Handled missing values where applicable.
   - Standardized date, category, and numerical formats.

3. **Feature Engineering**
   - Created a **Profit Margin (%)** metric to measure profitability efficiency.
   - Extracted **Year** from Order Date for time-based trend analysis.

4. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
   - Analyzed revenue, profit, order volume, customer segments, regions, and product categories.
   - Identified trends, growth patterns, and underperforming areas.

5. **Dashboard Development**
   - Built an interactive Excel dashboard with KPIs, charts, and slicers.
   - Enabled filtering by Year, Region, and Ship Mode.

---

## Key Insights
- **Total Revenue:** ~$2.3M  
- **Total Profit:** ~$286K  
- **Total Orders:** ~9,994  

### Revenue & Profit Trends
- Revenue and profit increased steadily year-over-year from 2014 to 2017.
- Profit growth outpaced revenue growth, indicating improved cost control and product mix.

### Regional Performance
- Highest-profit states include **California, New York, Texas, and Washington**.
- Revenue concentration aligns with high-density business and metropolitan regions.

### Product & Customer Insights
- **Technology** is the highest revenue and most profitable category.
- **Furniture** generates high revenue but low profit margins.
- **Consumer and Corporate segments** account for nearly 80% of total revenue.

### Discount Impact
- Higher discount levels correlate with reduced profit margins.
- Controlled discounting is critical for maintaining profitability in new markets.

---

## SMART Recommendations
- **Target metro business hubs** in Canada and Western Europe (Toronto, Vancouver, London, Berlin, Amsterdam).
- Focus expansion on **Technology and Office Supplies**, targeting a **20% revenue increase by Q2 2027**.
- Establish **regional distribution hubs** to reduce shipping time by 30% and logistics costs by 15%.
- Prioritize **Consumer and Corporate segments** in marketing strategy.
- Achieve **≥12% profit margin** in new markets by Q4 2028.
